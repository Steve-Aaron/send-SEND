Overview
We will design, build, host, and operate a neutral “Contact Your MP” campaign platform in response to the SEND consultation.
The platform will:
Be hosted on a standalone, neutral domain
Use non-Witherslack-specific branding
Be suitable for use by multiple independent schools / private providers
Allow organisations to link supporters to the same central campaign tool
The platform is intended to remain live throughout the 12-week consultation period.
Strategic Adjustment (March 3 Update)
Originally scoped as a Witherslack-branded microsite.
Now updated to:
Neutral branding (e.g. “Make Your Voice Heard” or similar)
No heavy client branding
Built to allow additional schools to link to it without rework
Avoid risk of rebuilding if additional stakeholders join
This reduces rebranding risk and increases reuse potential.
Core Platform Functionality
1. Campaign Microsite
Standalone neutral domain 
Mobile responsive
Clear primary CTA (e.g. “Write to Your MP” / “Make Your Voice Heard”)
Designed for rapid deployment
2. User Journey
Users will:
Enter name, email, postcode
Be matched to their correct MP 
Generate a pre-filled campaign email
Select their email provider (Gmail / Outlook / Other)
Open a pre-populated draft email in their chosen email client
Send the email from their own account
Email characteristics:
Core template fixed
Sent from the supporter’s own inbox
MPs reply directly to the supporter
